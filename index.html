Experience Castlepoint

Welcome to Castlepoint! Based less than 4 miles away from the beautiful sandy beaches of Bournemouth Castlepoint is the largest shopping park in the UK. Our selection of brands includes the very best of the high street, a few dining options, additional services for blue badge holders and ECR points we have over 3000 free car spaces which means you can enjoy your stay for as much as you want, we are green and dog friendly and make for a great destination for your weekend in Bournemouth.
